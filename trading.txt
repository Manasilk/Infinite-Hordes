Blacksmith:
- Gold >> Ingots
- Gold >> Weapon/Armour/Ammunition
- Ingots >> Gold
- Ingots + Gold >> Trims
Higher reputation reduces gold cost

Alchemist:
- Gold >> Herbs
- Gold >> Glass Bottle
Higher reputation reduces gold cost

Enchanter (By equipment slot):
 - Gold (Tier X) >> Level X<5 Enchants
 - Gold Ore + Lapis >> Emeralds
 - Echo Shard + Tier 4 Enchant >> Level 5 Enchants
Higher reputation reduces gold cost

Bakery:
 - Gold >> Bowl
 - Raw Food >> Cooked Food (Bread/Porkchop/Mutton/Rabbit/Cod/Salmon/Chicken)
 - Mushroom/Raw Rabbit + Bowl >> Mushroom/Rabbit Stew
Higher reputation reduces gold cost
